* Purpose:  
Minimal but secure Azure footprint for 1–2 compute components
(PaaS App Service OR 1–2 VMs).

* Ideal for:  
Small customers, PoCs, startups, single-application workloads.

* High-Level Requirements

1. Single workload
2. Minimal cost
3. Easy to manage
4. Security-first (Zero Trust Lite)
5. Support for both IaaS & PaaS compute
6. Scalable to S2/M1 patterns later
7. Landing zone aligned with CAF (lite version)
